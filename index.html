Crystals Collector Game

------------ Instructions ------------

A random number will be assigned to you at the start of the game.

As you can see below, there are four crystals. Click these crystals to reveal values added to your score.

You win by matching your score to the random number. You lose if your number goes over the random number.

The crystal values are reset every new game for an added challenge.
